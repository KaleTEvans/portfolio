Stock Dashboard

View GitHub

This is an ongoing side project I have been working on. Right now I have been pulling data from APIs such as the Yahoo Data API, AlphaVantage, and Stock News APIs. I believe the stock market lately has been heavily reactive to news and general sentiment, and I am trying to build a platform that can follow trends and sentiment and combine a bit of technical analysis to produce stock recommendations. I also plan on adding my own API to save the recommended stokcs and follow their price movement in order to see which analysis methods work the best and iterate on my methods to produce more accurate predictions.
